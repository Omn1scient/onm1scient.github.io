Decentralised social finance
the defi protocol

Decentralised transparency

Alice manages multiple sources of funding and tracks the achievement of goals transparently on the Blockchain. Citizens, corporates and governments worldwide can see on Alice what is achieved with private and public financing.

Multi-party coordination

All parties are created on Alice’s platform with set payout transactions and information rights. Alice’s open-source protocol automatically deploys the smart contracts that control these transactions amongst all parties.

Flexible governance

Social and environmental interventions may change over the course of a programme. Alice is the first company to build updatable smart-contracts to adjust project terms for all existing and new parties, without requiring new contractual agreements.

Privacy and security

Alice’s protocol securely encrypts and validates evidence of impact and allows parties to assign different access levels to user accounts. This way maximum data protection and security is enabled while minimising manual processing.

Defi Applications
manage payouts - track impact

  • Impact investment for everyone

    By automating RoI payouts to Investors and tracking impact on the Blockchain Alice brings impact investing to the masses.

  • Donations - reinvented

    Alice manages donations, transfers restricted funds and reports to donors the impact achieved with their money in real-time.

  • Legal - simplified

    Alice’s legal engine enables ID verification for all stakeholders and automatically generates legally binding agreements reflecting their transactions.

Our partners

Gnosis - ROI predirections

Alice secured a grant from Gnosis to tokenise impact investments, based on their conditional token, and to create an investors’ RoI prediction market.

ICL - Secure and open ID

Alice with ICL, secured an Innovate UK grant and created a self-sovereign ID for all parties to allow them to “selectively disclose” their data to others.

Skale - Fast tap and pay

Alice used Skale’s sidechain and won their award, at EthDenver, for Tappy - a tap and pay wallet that allows beneficiaries to self-validate impact.

Binance - Transparent impact

Binance is helping 1m young women live with dignity, offering them sanitary pads. Alice is tracking the impact on their lives validating proof transparently.

Ashurst - New era in legal

In partnership with Ashurst, Alice automates the creation of legally binding and fully compliant agreements between all stakeholders.

CAF - Tax exempt funding

Alice partnered with CAF to manage Income Tax Relief and exemption from Capital Gains Tax for the philanthropic funders on its platform.
